# Dice Roller (Android)

**Dice Roller** is a premium, highly interactive, and tactile Android application designed for board games, RPGs, and D&D sessions. It allows players to roll multiple dice of various sides with satisfying sensory feedback.

It is available on the Google Play Store: [Google Play Store Link](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=developer.android.vd.diceroller)

---

## 💎 Features

### 🎲 Multiple Dice & Types
- Roll anywhere from **1 to 9 dice** simultaneously.
- Supports diverse dice types: **D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, and D20** (advanced dice types lock/unlock via Pro status or watching a rewarded ad).

### 🔒 Yahtzee-Style Dice Locking
- Tap on any individual die to lock/unlock it. 
- Locked dice are visual highlighted with an accent border and lock badge.
- Locked dice retain their value and remain stationary during subsequent rolls, allowing for strategic tabletop gameplay.

### 📳 Immersive Audio-Tactile Feedback
- **Shake-to-Roll**: Natural shake gesture support using the device's accelerometer.
- **Audio Clack Synthesis**: High-fidelity, lightweight sound generation built on Android's `AudioTrack` API. It programmatically synthesizes staggered wooden clack sounds as each die lands.
- **Synced Haptic Vibrations**: Precise vibration clicks synced frame-perfectly with audio clacks to create a rich physical sensation.

### 🎨 Premium Themes & Dynamic Contrast
- Choose from a curated selection of sophisticated background themes including **Slate Dark (🌑)**, **Nordic Light (❄️)**, and soft pastels.
- Text, icon buttons, selector chips, and card containers dynamically adjust their contrast and drawables to look stunning in both light and dark backgrounds.
- Lifetime Pro unlocks custom color tints for the dice faces.

### 📜 Visual Roll History
- Displays a visual ledger of past rolls.
- Shows custom miniature colored dice vectors indicating the exact values rolled instead of simple plain text.

## 🛠️ Settings & Configurations
Customize your experience through the settings menu:
* **Background Color**: Select your favorite background palette.
* **Dice Theme**: Toggle custom dice colors (Premium).
* **Show Total**: Show or hide the sum of the dice values.
* **Enable Vibration**: Turn tactile haptic feedback on or off.
* **Shake to Roll**: Turn gesture-based rolling on or off.
* **Sound Effects**: Toggle synthesized clack sounds.
